Services

LITIGATION

Our lawyers are recognised as leaders in various aspects of litigation. The team operates across key sectors both nationally and internationally, dealing with disputes through the courts or through arbitration and adjudication.

ARBITRATION

We conduct arbitration of dispute in many field of commercial activities. We are well aware of the cost and delay involved in litigation through the courts. Arbitration provides a fast and clean route through the legal maze.

PROPERTY PRACTICE

The team combines technical legal expertise with commercial awareness in providing advice on both Conveyancing and Commercial Property transactions. Our property experts are “gifted” with special abilities in risk shifting mechanisms for the benefit of our clients.

DEBT RECOVERY

For more than 20 years, we have successfully provided debt collection services in the UK, Nigeria, Middle East and many other countries around the world. Using a wide range of legal techniques, we help companies to recover their money, regardless of the location of their debtors.

OIL AND GAS

The team has lawyers who have advised oil companies operating in oil producing States of Nigeria. We are able to advise clients both in upstream and downstream areas of the oil & gas industry.

IMMIGRATION & NATIONALITY

This team undertakes complicated and specialist area of immigration and nationality law.
