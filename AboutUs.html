About us

We are delighted to introduce our law firm, Hackman & Co. which's an extension of Hackman Solicitors based in London since 2001. We now have branches, in Lagos, Ibadan and Benin. We offer a range of legal services to both companies and individuals. In all the areas of law that we specialize in Hackman takes pride in delivering top notch service to our clients. Specifically we provide assistance in the following areas; debt recovery, project finance, banking, taxation, mergers and acquisitions corporate matters, commercial law, dispute resolution services, oil and gas sector expertise private client matters including litigation support or advice, on estate related issues.

Our vision

Our mission is to excel in the field of legal services by consistently delivering the highest professional standards. We are dedicated to providing our clients with effective and innovative solutions across all areas of the law. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every aspect of our practice, from the expertise of our legal professionals to the quality of our client service. We understand that legal matters can be complex and challenging, and we are here to navigate these intricacies with skill and precision.

Our Approach

Research

The primary objective of Our research is to gain a deep understanding of client needs and expectations to enhance the quality of legal services provided by Hackman Solicitor. We utilize a mixed-method approach, combining qualitative and quantitative research methods. Conduct client interviews, surveys, and feedback sessions to gather insights. Review historical client cases and outcomes for patterns and trends.

Strategy

Outline specific objectives aligned with the research findings and the vision of becoming a client-centric law firm. Prioritize objectives that focus on enhancing communication, transparency, and the overall client experience. We develop a detailed implementation plan that includes specific initiatives, timelines, responsible parties, and resources required for each strategic objective.

Design

Highlight the significance of design in creating a positive and memorable client experience that complements the firm's solicitation and mediation expertise. We develop a client engagement strategy that includes regular communications, feedback mechanisms, and opportunities for clients to provide input.
